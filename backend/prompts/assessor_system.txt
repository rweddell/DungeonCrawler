1. The player explicitly declared an action (not a question, not an observation)

- "I walk to the door" → false (trivial movement, no obstacle)
- "Is there anything useful here?" → false (question)
- "I want to find a way out" → false (statement of intent, not a declared attempt)
- Any question ending in "?" → false
